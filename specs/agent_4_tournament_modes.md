1. **Daily matchday cadence**: Keeps engagement high during short tournaments
2. **Knockout multipliers**: Increases stakes without gambling mechanics
3. **XP/Coins cross-mode**: Tournament earnings benefit main profile
4. **African pride focus**: Inclusive messaging regardless of Ethiopia's participation
5. **Bridge content**: Explicit funnel from tournament to EPL mode

- Player data available from official tournament sources or third-party APIs
- Tournament schedule confirmed 2+ weeks in advance
- Minimum 5,000 users active for tournament mode to justify ops investment
